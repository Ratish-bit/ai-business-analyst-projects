Project Title: AI-Assisted Business Reporting & Decision Support System

Objective:
To demonstrate how data analytics and generative AI can be used to automate business reporting and support faster, data-driven decision-making.

Business Problem:
Manual reporting delays insights for leadership. This project shows how SQL, Power BI, and AI can streamline reporting workflows.

Tools & Technologies:
SQL, Power BI, Microsoft Excel, Generative AI, Prompt Engineering

Work Performed:
- Designed a structured dataset to simulate business sales data
- Used SQL queries to analyze revenue, profit, and performance trends
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights
- Created AI prompts to generate executive summaries and recommendations

Key Insights:
- Identified top-performing regions and products
- Analyzed monthly revenue and profit trends
- Highlighted areas for marketing and operational improvement

Outcome:
Demonstrated an AI-assisted reporting workflow that improves insight generation speed and decision quality for business stakeholders.
